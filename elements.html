Volunteer Process

Overview

Once you sign up, you'll receive a brief orientation and training session. Our team will walk you through the tools and materials needed to help with formatting, delivery, or story collection.

Responsibilities

Book Formatting: Help design and layout memory books.
Delivery: Bring books to seniors at care homes.
Story Collection: Engage with families to gather stories and photos.

Impact

Your work helps bring joy to seniors and their families by preserving treasured memories and delivering personalized keepsakes.

Next Steps

After signing up, we will reach out to you with available opportunities and details on how to get started.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the typical time commitment?

Volunteers typically spend 2-4 hours a week, depending on their availability and chosen role.

Do I need specific skills to volunteer?

No specific skills are required. Training is provided, and roles are flexible depending on your strengths.

How do I start?

Simply sign up through our form, and we will reach out to guide you through the next steps.
