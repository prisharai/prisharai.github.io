Our Mission

Bringing joy and preserving memories through personalized memory books for seniors.

About Stories to Smiles

Stories to Smiles is a community-driven initiative where families share their loved onesâ€™ stories, and volunteers help transform these into beautiful memory books. Our goal is to strengthen connections between generations and bring happiness to seniors through tangible memories.

Inspiration Behind the Project

Inspired by the importance of preserving family history, Stories to Smiles was founded to help seniors relive their cherished moments. This project began with a desire to bring warmth and connection to those who may feel isolated, especially in senior care homes.
